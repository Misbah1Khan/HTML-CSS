Cricket

Cricket is a widely popular sport played in numerous countries around the world, particularly in countries such as India, England, Australia, and Pakistan. It is a bat-and-ball game that involves two teams of eleven players each. The game is played on a large oval-shaped field with a rectangular pitch in the center. The objective is for the batting team to score as many runs as possible while the fielding team tries to dismiss the batsmen and restrict the scoring.

Cricket is known for its unique blend of strategy, skill, and endurance. Matches can last multiple days in the Test format, or shorter durations in limited-overs formats like One-Day Internationals (ODIs) and Twenty20 (T20) matches. The sport is characterized by a variety of exciting elements, such as powerful batting strokes, precision bowling techniques, acrobatic fielding, and intense rivalries between teams.
