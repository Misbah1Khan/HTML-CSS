CHICKEN PULAO

Chicken Pulao is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish that perfectly blends tender chicken with fragrant spices and basmati rice. This beloved dish is a popular choice for gatherings, celebrations, or simply as a comforting meal. Succulent pieces of chicken are marinated in a blend of yogurt, ginger, garlic, and a medley of spices, allowing the flavors to infuse into the meat. The chicken is then cooked together with basmati rice, creating a harmonious combination of flavors. The rice absorbs the rich essence of the chicken and spices, resulting in each grain being infused with a delightful aroma and taste. Chicken Pulao is often garnished with fried onions, raisins, and cashews, adding a touch of sweetness and crunch to the dish. This one-pot wonder is a complete meal in itself and is best enjoyed with raita (yogurt sauce) or a side of salad. Chicken Pulao is a versatile and satisfying dish that is sure to please both chicken lovers and rice enthusiasts alike.
