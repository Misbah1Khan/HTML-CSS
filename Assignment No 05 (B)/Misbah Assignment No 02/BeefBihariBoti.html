BEEF BIHARI BOTI

Beef Bihari Boti is a delectable and flavorsome grilled beef dish that originates from the Bihari region of Pakistan. This popular barbecue delicacy features tender pieces of beef that are marinated in a spiced yogurt mixture for several hours, allowing the flavors to penetrate the meat. The marinade typically includes a blend of spices like papaya paste, ginger, garlic, cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and a touch of mustard oil. This combination of spices creates a robust and savory flavor profile. The marinated beef is then skewered and grilled to perfection, resulting in juicy and charred pieces of meat that are infused with a smoky and aromatic essence. Beef Bihari Boti is often served with naan bread, mint chutney, and a squeeze of lemon, enhancing the overall experience. The tender beef, combined with the flavorful marinade and the charred texture from the grilling process, makes Beef Bihari Boti a favorite choice for meat lovers and a star attraction at barbecue gatherings and festive occasions.
