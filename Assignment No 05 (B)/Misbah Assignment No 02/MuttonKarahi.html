MUTTON KARAHI

Mutton Karahi is a delectable and aromatic dish that captures the essence of Pakistani and North Indian cuisine. This flavorful delicacy showcases tender mutton cooked in a rich and robust tomato-based gravy. The mutton is marinated with a blend of spices, including cumin, coriander, turmeric, red chili powder, and garlic, allowing the flavors to permeate the meat and enhance its succulence. It is then stir-fried with onions, tomatoes, and a medley of aromatic spices, creating a harmonious blend of flavors. The result is a dish that boasts tender mutton immersed in a thick and flavorful gravy that tantalizes the taste buds. Mutton Karahi is often garnished with fresh coriander leaves, ginger slices, and green chilies, adding a refreshing and vibrant touch. This hearty and indulgent dish pairs perfectly with naan bread or steamed rice, allowing you to savor every mouthful. Mutton Karahi is a true culinary delight that showcases the rich and complex flavors of mutton, making it a cherished choice for meat enthusiasts and lovers of bold and aromatic dishes.
