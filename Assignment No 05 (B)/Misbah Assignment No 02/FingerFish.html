FINGER FISH

Finger Fish, also known as Fish Fingers, is a popular and appetizing dish that features bite-sized pieces of fish coated in a crispy and flavorful batter. This delightful finger food is loved by seafood enthusiasts and makes for a perfect appetizer or snack. The fish used for Finger Fish can vary, but commonly it is made with white fish fillets like cod or haddock. The fish is cut into small strips or chunks, marinated with a combination of spices, lemon juice, and sometimes a touch of garlic and ginger for added flavor. The marinated fish pieces are then coated in a seasoned batter, usually made with a mixture of flour, breadcrumbs, and spices, giving them a crispy and golden texture when deep-fried or baked. The result is a plate of Finger Fish that is crispy on the outside, while the fish inside remains tender and flaky. Finger Fish is often served with a side of tartar sauce or a zesty dip, along with a squeeze of lemon for a tangy kick. Whether enjoyed as a standalone appetizer or as part of a seafood platter, Finger Fish is a delightful treat that brings together the delicate flavors of fish with a satisfying crunch, making it a favorite choice for seafood lovers and a popular addition to any menu.
