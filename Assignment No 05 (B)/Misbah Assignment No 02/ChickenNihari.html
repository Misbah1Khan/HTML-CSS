CHICKEN NIHARI

Chicken Nihari is a hearty and aromatic dish that is deeply rooted in the culinary traditions of Pakistan and India. This slow-cooked delicacy features tender chicken simmered in a flavorful gravy enriched with a blend of spices. The chicken is cooked on low heat for several hours, allowing the flavors to meld together and the meat to become incredibly tender. The rich and velvety gravy of Chicken Nihari is infused with a medley of spices such as ginger, garlic, cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg, resulting in a robust and aromatic flavor profile. The dish is traditionally garnished with fried onions, fresh ginger, chopped cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon, adding a burst of freshness to the rich and flavorful gravy. Chicken Nihari is often enjoyed as a special treat during breakfast or served as a centerpiece dish during festive occasions. This indulgent and satisfying dish is best enjoyed with naan bread or steamed rice, allowing you to savor every spoonful of its tantalizing flavors.
